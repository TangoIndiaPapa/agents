# AI Warning

This repository contains content created or assisted by AI systems.

## Important Notice
- AI output can be incorrect, incomplete, outdated, or misleading.
- AI output can introduce security, legal, compliance, privacy, or licensing risks.
- AI-generated recommendations are not a substitute for human review.

## Required Human Review
Before use in production, maintainers must perform:
- Technical validation (correctness, reliability, and performance)
- Security validation (threat modeling, dependency and secret checks)
- Legal and licensing validation
- Policy and compliance validation

## Accountability
Final responsibility for all repository content remains with human maintainers and approvers.
